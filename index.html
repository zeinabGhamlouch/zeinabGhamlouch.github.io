Who I am

I am interested in the intersection of large language models, knowledge representation, and structured semantic systems. My work explores how neural and symbolic ideas can be combined to build AI that is both powerful and interpretable.

What I work on

I currently focus on taxonomy-related tasks including enrichment, expansion, and induction, with an emphasis on semantic hierarchy, representation learning, and intelligent knowledge organization.
